About Us

In today's world, drones are a key assests for human safety. They reduce human error and provide a fast and effecient way to deliver medical products to places where it would be difficult to reach. With the M.A.V.I initiative we plan to cut cost, time and human error. The drone flys automatically to the spot and uses facial recognition to land next to the patient.
