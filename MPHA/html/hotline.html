M.A.V.I

The M.A.V.I is short for medical aerial vehicle initiative, this drone is designed to get into tight places where sometime medical authorities can't reach. We believe it should be deployed in war zones and 3rd world countries where paved roads aren't accessible. By doing that we could potentially save the lives of thousands of people trapped in risky situations.
